<h1>BetterDocs Plugin: Quick Installation and Configuration Guide</h1>

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Overview</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Install, activate, and configure the <span class="highlight">BetterDocs</span> WordPress plugin to publish a structured knowledge base quickly. Learn prerequisites, installation (dashboard and manual), initial setup, creating docs, using shortcodes and widgets, and key settings for search, templates, and access control.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Prerequisites</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>WordPress site running a supported version (WordPress 5.8+ recommended)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Administrator access to the WordPress dashboard</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>PHP 7.4+ and MySQL/MariaDB per modern WordPress requirements</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Recommended: a staging site to test changes before going live</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Install the <span class="highlight">BetterDocs</span> Plugin</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Method 1: Install from WordPress Dashboard (Recommended)</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>In WordPress Admin, go to <strong>Plugins</strong> → <strong>Add New</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Search for <em>BetterDocs</em> by typing <code>BetterDocs</code> in the search bar.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Locate the plugin named <strong>BetterDocs – Best Documentation &amp; Knowledge Base Plugin</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Click <strong>Install Now</strong>, then click <strong>Activate</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Method 2: Manual Upload (ZIP File)</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Download the latest <em>BetterDocs</em> ZIP from <a href="https://wordpress.org/plugins/betterdocs/">https://wordpress.org/plugins/betterdocs/</a> or the vendor site if you have Pro.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>In WordPress Admin, go to <strong>Plugins</strong> → <strong>Add New</strong> → <strong>Upload Plugin</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Choose the ZIP file and click <strong>Install Now</strong>, then <strong>Activate</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Activate and Run the <span class="highlight">Setup Wizard</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>After activation, you may be prompted to launch the BetterDocs Setup Wizard.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>If prompted, click <strong>Run the Setup Wizard</strong>. Otherwise, navigate to <strong>BetterDocs</strong> → <strong>Settings</strong> to configure manually.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Follow the on-screen steps to:<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Create the main <em>Docs</em> page (the archive page where your knowledge base appears)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Choose a template layout (grid, list, category-first, etc.)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Enable the <em>Instant Answer</em> search for live suggestions (optional)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Import sample content (optional)</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list --></li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Create Your First <span class="highlight">Doc</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Go to <strong>BetterDocs</strong> → <strong>Add New</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Enter a clear title that matches a user task or question.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Write content using the Block Editor (Gutenberg) or Classic Editor as configured.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Assign the doc to one or more <strong>Categories</strong> to group topics logically.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Set a <strong>Featured Image</strong> if your template uses thumbnails.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Click <strong>Publish</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Organize with <span class="highlight">Categories</span> and Tags</h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Navigate to <strong>BetterDocs</strong> → <strong>Categories</strong> to add, edit, and reorder categories.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use descriptive names and short slugs for clean URLs.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Optionally assign <strong>parent</strong> categories to build a hierarchy.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use tags sparingly to connect related topics across categories.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Configure Essential <span class="highlight">Settings</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Access all options at <strong>BetterDocs</strong> → <strong>Settings</strong>. Key tabs and controls include:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">General</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Docs Page</strong>: Select the primary archive page for your knowledge base.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Permalinks</strong>: Choose readable slugs (e.g., <code>/docs/</code>) and save permalinks under <strong>Settings</strong> → <strong>Permalinks</strong> if prompted.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Breadcrumbs</strong>: Enable to show navigation paths for users.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Comments</strong>: Allow/disable comments on docs based on your support process.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Layout</span> &amp; Design</h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Archive Template</strong>: Grid, list, or category-first layout.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Single Doc Template</strong>: Choose header style, sidebar position, and related articles block.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Sidebar</strong>: Toggle table of contents (auto-generated from headings) and category navigation.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Search and <span class="highlight">Instant Answer</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Live Search</strong>: Enable suggestions as users type.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Search Scope</strong>: Limit results to selected categories if needed.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>FAQ/Chat-like Drawer</strong> (Instant Answer): Configure the floating button, trigger, and branding.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Content <span class="highlight">Analytics</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Enable pageview tracking and helpfulness feedback to measure performance.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>View reports under <strong>BetterDocs</strong> → <strong>Analytics</strong> (availability may vary by edition).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Access Control</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Restrict docs or categories to logged-in users or specific roles (Pro feature).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use private docs for internal teams and public docs for customers.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Shortcodes and <span class="highlight">Blocks</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use Gutenberg blocks (e.g., Docs List, Category Grid) from the block inserter.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Shortcodes can output archives, categories, and search in classic editors or page builders.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Display Your Knowledge Base</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Using Gutenberg Blocks</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list {"ordered":true} -->
<ol class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Edit the page where you want to display docs (e.g., <em>Docs</em> or <em>Help Center</em>).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Add a <strong>BetterDocs</strong> block such as <em>Category Grid</em>, <em>Doc List</em>, or <em>Search</em>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Adjust block settings (columns, category filters, show icons, etc.).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Update or publish the page and preview.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ol>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading">Using <span class="highlight">Shortcodes</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Place shortcodes in any page, post, or widget area. Examples:</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:code -->
<pre class="wp-block-code"><code>&#91;betterdocs_category_grid columns="3" include="getting-started,account,billing"]</code></pre>
<!-- /wp:code -->

<!-- wp:shortcode -->
[betterdocs_search placeholder="Search the knowledge base..."]
<!-- /wp:shortcode -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>[betterdocs_list category="getting-started" posts_per_page="10"]

</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:paragraph -->
<p>Parameters vary by version; consult the shortcode reference in the plugin’s documentation.</p>
<!-- /wp:paragraph -->

<!-- wp:heading {"level":3} -->
<h3 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Widgets and Menus</span></h3>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Add BetterDocs widgets to sidebars or footers under <strong>Appearance</strong> → <strong>Widgets</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Link your Docs page in site navigation via <strong>Appearance</strong> → <strong>Menus</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Style and <span class="highlight">Branding</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Match your theme using built-in color and typography controls (Layout/Design settings).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Override templates or add custom CSS in <strong>Appearance</strong> → <strong>Customize</strong> → <strong>Additional CSS</strong>.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>For advanced customization, check if your theme builder or page builder has BetterDocs integrations.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Migration and <span class="highlight">Import</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use the built-in importer (when available) to bring content from other KB plugins.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>For WordPress-native imports, go to <strong>Tools</strong> → <strong>Import</strong> and map categories afterward.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Validate permalinks and redirects to avoid broken links.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Performance and SEO</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Enable caching and a CDN for faster doc loading.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use clear titles and H2/H3 headings; BetterDocs supports a <span class="highlight">Table of Contents</span> for scannability.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Configure SEO metadata using plugins like Yoast or Rank Math; exclude low-value archive pages if necessary.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Submit your Docs sitemap in Google Search Console for indexing.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<h2 class="wp-block-heading">Roles and <span class="highlight">Workflow</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Define who creates, reviews, and publishes docs (e.g., Author → Editor → Admin).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Use draft and pending review statuses; schedule updates for product releases.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li>Enable feedback widgets to collect “Was this helpful?” ratings and comments (Pro feature may apply).</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->

<!-- wp:heading -->
<h2 class="wp-block-heading"><span class="highlight">Troubleshooting</span></h2>
<!-- /wp:heading -->

<!-- wp:list -->
<ul class="wp-block-list"><!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Docs page shows 404</strong>: Visit <strong>Settings</strong> → <strong>Permalinks</strong> and click <strong>Save</strong> to flush rewrite rules.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Styles look broken</strong>: Clear caches (plugin, theme, CDN) and verify no CSS minification conflicts.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Blocks not appearing</strong>: Ensure the plugin is active and your WordPress version supports the block editor.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Search not returning results</strong>: Reindex if your search plugin requires it; confirm categories are included and docs are published.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item -->

<!-- wp:list-item -->
<li><strong>Access restricted unexpectedly</strong>: Review role-based visibility settings and any membership plugins.</li>
<!-- /wp:list-item --></ul>
<!-- /wp:list -->
